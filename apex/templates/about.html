About Apex Construction

A decade and a half of building Kenya's future — one project at a time. Learn who we are, what drives us, and the talented team behind every structure we build.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Apex Construction Limited was founded with a single purpose: to deliver construction services that set a new standard for quality, reliability, and professionalism in Kenya. Starting as a small local contractor in Kisumu, we have grown into a full-service construction company with a portfolio spanning residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects across the country.

Our growth has been built on repeat business and referrals — the highest form of client endorsement. Every project we undertake reflects our unwavering commitment to safety, quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and delivering results that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that transform communities and create lasting value for our clients.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative construction company in East Africa, recognized for quality and integrity.
